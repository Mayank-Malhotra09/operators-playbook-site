Opinions, locked

This playbook takes positions.
Here are four of them.

Most courses hedge so they're never wrong. Hedged advice is useless when you're staring at Ads Manager with money on the line. These stances are baked into every chapter.

S/01

CAPI is mandatory from day one — on partnered integrations.

Set up the Conversions API through your platform's partnered integration before your first rupee of spend. And don't layer manual events on top of it — duplicate signal isn't extra signal, it's broken signal.

Non-negotiable
S/02

Meta under-reporting vs Shopify by 10–15% is normal.

Stop panicking about it. The number that should scare you is the opposite one: if Meta claims more revenue than your store actually made, your setup is broken — and you're optimizing on fiction.

Calibrate to this
S/03

AEM is obsolete for the standard DTC operator.

Aggregated Event Measurement made sense in a specific post-iOS14 moment. If a tutorial is teaching you to rank events in 2026, it's teaching you archaeology. Skip it and spend that hour on signal quality instead.

Stop learning this
S/04

Andromeda rewards clean account structure.

Meta's retrieval system works best when your account gives it clear, consolidated signal — not seventeen overlapping ad sets fighting each other. Structure is a performance lever now, not housekeeping.
